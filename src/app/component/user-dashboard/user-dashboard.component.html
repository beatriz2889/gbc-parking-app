Welcome to the GBC Parking Reservation App. Here are a few important things to note:

Make a Reservation

You can reserve a parking spot at any of the three parking lots available at Casa Loma Campus. You may only reserve a spot if the spot is not taken and for a maximum time of 2 hours. You will be required to pay for the reservation at the time of confirmation. There is an option to extend your reservation, but only if there are no current reservations at the specified time and only up to a maximum time of 2 hours. Additional payment will be required if you choose to extend

Cancellation

You may choose to cancel your reservation only if your reservation time has not yet started. The process is irreversible and your payment will be refunded.

Late Fees

In the event that you stay past your scheduled time, you will be charged a late fee. You can click on the "late fees" link to view the amount owed.


We hope you will enjoy using the app!
